Our Campus

BFU is th biggest & first College-cum-University of our city, Bahraich. It is spread across 200 acres with cycles and electric scooters for students to travel inside the university. We have a beautiful Gardern, a park, sports fields and all required laboratories with full equipments. We organise multiple cultural and social events for our students throughout the year.
