UPCOMING PROJECTS:


EVERYTHING is the first part of the two part movie event. It follows the story of a man Shiv and his friend Vaibhav. The movie revolves around their life growing up together and dealing with their problems. Shiv is a middle class boy who wants to become a doctor to make his parents and Vaibhav proud and also to marry his crush, Sania. This story is filled with fun , emotion , love and VIOLENCE. This movie also shows the corruption in modern day politics and judicial system and how very few good people are fighting for the good of everyone.

Part Two :NOTHING follows the aftermath of everything that happened and was hinted at in the first part. The country is going through a political turmoil as people of different communities are rioting for the unstoppable murders happening everywhere in the state. This part will reveal how Shiv and Vaibhav and their family are inter-connected with all that is happening and what that means for their lives.
